Laser Hair Removal

At Temptation Beauty London we offer the best laser hair removal option which using

SHR Technology.

Super Hair Removal ( SHR ) uses low energy light via rapid pulses to safely traumatize the stem cells of any unwanted hair, helping prevent any regrowth. Compared to older, traditional hair removal treatments that use Laser and IPL, our Super Hair Removal is faster, gentler and more effective releasing 20-30 shots per second, while IPL releases only 1.

The treatment can take as little as 5 minutes, depending on the treated area. For example a full face will normally take 15 minutes, while both legs take an average of 30 minutes.

IS SHR technology suitable for all skin/hair types? Can I have it at any time of the year?

Double YES, it works amazing on all skin and hair types and it is possible to have your treatment done even during the summer when tans have developed.
